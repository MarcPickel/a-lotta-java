Tolle Lege! Take Up and Read!

For men, the challenge of taking up and reading a book whittles down to three obstacles: Boredom, Isolation, and Not Knowing the Value of a Good Book.

We overcome these obstacles head-on by tackling good books with jovial camaraderie and simplicity. The goal isn't to just "get through books" but to read them in a way that forms us through the gift of friendship. Take up and read!
